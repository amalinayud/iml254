Reading Novels

Here some recommendations from me. These three novels are from most of my favorite author

King Haidar by Anjell

A tale of fate and destiny where two souls find their paths entwined in an epic journey of love and power.

Pengganti Paling Setia by Nina Fini

Dive into the emotional depths of loyalty and resilience as characters navigate through the complexities of life and love.

She by Lollysnow

Embark on an unforgettable adventure with vibrant characters in a world where fantasy and reality intertwine seamlessly.

Indulge your imagination and discover new realms within these pages!

Brisk Walk for Stress Relief

Walking is an easy way to rejuvenate your mind and body:

  • Take a leisurely stroll through beautiful parks and peaceful paths.
  • Breathe in fresh air while listening to nature's relaxing sounds.
  • Allow every step to melt away stress and concerns, leaving you feeling refreshed and energised.
  • Enjoy the rhythm of your steps as you reconnect with your inner peace.

Step outside, clear your mind, and embrace the beauty of the world around you!
